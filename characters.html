Clare von Eisenburg

A mysterious girl who doesn't seem very human. When you first met her, she was on the verge of starving to death, and threatened to kill you if you didn't give her food. After feeding her "earth food," she became nicer, but she still threatens to kill you if you don't give her food when she's hungry.

Katherine Aizawa

Your little sister. She loves you, but she tries not to show it, and instead attempts to bully you. Loves unicorns, but hates being treated like a child. She's always calling you a "baka" (idiot), but she doesn't mean it, so don't get hurt. Definitely a tsundere.

Jenny Sakura

The class president of your class; also your childhood friend. She's extremely nice and caring, and offers to make you bentos. She gets easily gets embarrassed and blushes, looking like a red tomato. She's pretty popular amongst everyone, and gets everyone's attention, but perhaps the only attention she wants is from you...?

Laura Yoshida

Your kouhai. She's pretty mysterious, and pops up in front of you out of nowhere. Whispering "senpai...", she's always staying close-by. Sometimes, you catch her staring at you from the corner of the room, or hiding behind a pole. When you try to speak to her, she runs away. But she wants your love. And only your love. If you let her go, something bad might happen... Definitely a yandere.
