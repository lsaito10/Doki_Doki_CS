Doki Doki CS Club

Doki Doki CS Club is a visual novel, both with romance and a bit of horror. You, as the protagonist of the story, have to choose between the available options on the screen to either have a good or bad ending with your waifu.

Your first job is to complete the Your Waifu quiz, to determine which heroine is best fit for you. Remember to fill out your name. After that, you're on your own. Good luck...

Don't blink. Never close your eyes. If you want to survive, don't let your eyes pop...
